About the Park

A best-in-class industrial development at the crossroads of international commerce

Excellence in Industrial Development

Capote International Business Park is a 300-acre, Class A commercial development positioned in the heart of South Texas. Located in the City of Pharr, this exceptional development is adjacent to the most active commercial port of entry in the Rio Grande Valley.

Originally owned and developed by the Dyer family (Phase 1: Buildings A-F), the industrial park is on its second phase of development with recently completed Buildings G and H on the east side of the park.

Capote Phase II offers space only for lease, ensuring the highest quality and standards through single source responsibility, ownership, development and management.
